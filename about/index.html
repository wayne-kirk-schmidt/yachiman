About the Hall - 10,000 Arrows

10,000 Arrows is a quiet hall for exploring haiku in English. We approach each poem on its own terms, with appreciation and reflection. The hall combines the practices of haiku and Kyūdō at Sanjūsangen-dō.

Kyūdō Sanjūsangen-dō

Curated by Wayne Kirk Schmidt . The work here is part of a longer practice in language, systems, and attention. The result is building places where we can explore quietly and without demand.
