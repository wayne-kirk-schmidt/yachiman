About the Hall

10,000 Arrows is a quiet hall for haiku.

Each poem is encountered on its own terms — without search, without urgency, without instruction.

The hall draws from traditions of attention and intention, including haiku practice and Kyūdō.

Kyūdō Sanjūsangen-dō

About the Author

This hall is curated by Wayne Kirk Schmidt

The work here is part of a longer practice in language, systems, and attention.

The result is building places where we can explore quietly and without demand.
